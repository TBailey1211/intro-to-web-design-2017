Products and Services we provide:

  • A wide variety of flowers freshly cut and expertly arranged: Perfect for weddings, gifts, prom night, etc.
  • Tropical plants
  • Green plants: tasteful and a good choice for condolences at funerals, a natural addition to a wedding, or simply for display in a home
  • Seasonal decorations: Holiday arrangements and accessories for any special occasion (Christmas, Birthday, Baby Shower, Valentine’s Day, etc.)
  • Floral arrangements
  • Wedding arrangements and decorations (bouquets, centerpieces, floral arrangements for the venue, etc.)
  • Funeral designs/casket sprays
  • Gift baskets
  • Custom corsages and boutonnieres
  • Gift cards, candles, vases, and a wide selection of pottery

In addition to live flowers and greenery, Bamberg Floral Supplies also provides artificial arrangements.

Occasions:

We have a range of specific occasions and holidays that we provide pre-made, customizable arrangements for at a generally lower cost. These are eligible for same-day delivery in most cases and are available in both live and artificial arrangements.

Delivery

We provide delivery services to multiple local funeral homes, hospitals, rehabilitation centers, retirement homes, nursing homes, and wedding venues. We will also provide specific deliveries to various establishments and private residences upon request.

Costs

Prices of flowers and arrangements range from $45-$150 dollars based on client requests. Additional special services are subject to additional cost. Our delivery services are complimentary within a 50-mile radius of our business location. Out of town/state deliveries are welcome and are subject to additional cost.
