Privacy Policy/Your rights as a customer

Bamberg Floral Supplies is dedicated to the protection of customer privacy and has a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you find yourself unsatisfied with our services, please contact us at (803) 245-4326.

Order change policy:

We offer changes to orders at no additional cost to you as well as refunds if you are not completely satisfied with your purchase upon in-store pick-up. Please be aware that any orders that have already been delivered or are en-route to be delivered are not subject to cancellation or refund. Cancelations or change requests must be filed either in person or over the phone; we do not accept cancelations or change requests via e-mail. In the event of an address change for an order, please contact our number provided.

Customer Security

When purchasing goods or services from us, we will require certain information such as your name and contact info for purposes of service and completing the transaction. Personal information collected about persons for arrangements as well as individual customer information will not be shared for any purpose and will remain secure. E-mails and correspondence from customers may be collected and used to troubleshoot issues with disputes or Terms of Service.

Consent as a Customer

The information contained within this privacy statement is subject to change. Please be aware that these changes can and will occur. Regular monitoring of this page on your part as a customer will ensure no misunderstandings between you as a customer and our business. By using our website and visiting our store, you are consenting to the use and collection of your information as stated within this Privacy Statement.
