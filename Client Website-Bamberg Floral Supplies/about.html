About Us:

What is Bamberg Floral Supplies?

Bamberg Floral Supplies is a local florist that is well known across the Lowcountry. With overwhelmingly positive feedback, 5-star ratings, and many satisfied customers, this shop offers a wide variety of flowers and plants to suit every need. From weddings to funerals to anything in between, Bamberg Floral Supplies will be able to provide. Bamberg Floral Supplies also offers additional services in the creation of arrangements and bouquets for numerous types of occasions. All supplies and plants are grown locally within the state. This floral supply store is owned and operated by Mrs. Marilyn Cooler.

About the owner

Mrs. Marilyn Cooler has been a resident of Bamberg County all of her life and is extremely devoted to serving her community. Bamberg Floral Supplies is not just a business venture, but also a way for her to give back to the community by providing a floral arrangement service. Every town needs a good, reliable florist that they can trust, and Cooler was determined to be that particular someone. In addition to her work as a florist, Cooler is also a devoted wife and mother with nothing but love and adoration for her family. The care she gives to her family as well as attention to detail within her own home is reflected in the flowers and arrangements that Bamberg Floral Supplies provides, producing nothing but the highest quality for each and every customer. Satisfaction is guaranteed with the amount of love and care that every single flower receives.
